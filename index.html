My name is Jonathan Sweeney. I am a PhD student in economics at UH-Manoa, and economic research assistant at NOAA Fisheries. I work on topics in applied econometrics, Bayesian methods, and natural resource economics. My recent dissertation work has been on predicting the effects of policy changes on Hawaii's fishing industry.
